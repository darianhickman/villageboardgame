Village the Game

Welcome to Village the Game Prototype: "Kenya"

You are an entrepreneur in Kenya starting businesses in your village to improve the lives of the villagers. For example, start Kickstart to sell irrigation pumps to farmers. Start Kiva to help villagers get credit so they can buy your other tools.

How to Win:

Have the most sales revenue once all the villager needs have been met.

How to Play

Each Player rolls dice to move around the village. During your turn, if you land on a business you can start that business. When your opponents land on your business they buy tools and services from your business and use them to improve the village. If you get all the businesses of the same color, you can upgrade your businesses and make the village even stronger.

Turn Order

  1. Roll dice to see where you land.
  2. If you land on an unowned business, buy it or let another player start it.
  3. If you land on a Villager space, find out what the Villagers need.
  4. If you land on a News space, find out what is happening in the Village
  5. Trade money and businesses with other players
  6. Buy and Sell upgrades
  7. End Turn

Notes:

There isn't real multiplayer support yet. You and friends have to play it together on one computer.

Warning:

If you refresh your browser, the current game will be erased.

*"Kenya" does not work in Internet Explorer; use Firefox, Safari or Google Chrome. Thank you!*
